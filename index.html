ESA

Emotional Support Animals are a companion animal that provides therapeutic benefits to individuals with mental or emotional disabilities. Unlike service animals, ESAs do not require specialized training to perform specific tasks. Their primary role is to offer comfort and companionship, which can help alleviate symptoms associated with various mental health conditions.

Dogs and Cats

While all types of animals are used as an emotional support animal, dogs and cats are the main ones.
